Conversation Tips

Getting a conversation going can be difficult, consider these tips to help you along.

Ask What then Why

People want to connect and don't love small talk. Ask your conversation partner(s) a what question followed by a sincere why questions. Consider this example:

Brian: So, what do you study?

Anna: I am a physics major.

Brian: Cool! Why are you interested in physics?

Listen, Listen, Listen!

Focus on listening to your conversation partner(s) instead of thinking about what you are going to say next.
